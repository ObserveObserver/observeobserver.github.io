come on, let’s do something nice. let’s give each other the love n affection we’ve missed out on. let’s try to alleviate each other’s loneliness. let’s make each others life on earth less miserable. it’s okay to hurt, it’s okay to be sad, it’s okay to feel empty; we’ll all be happy one day, even if it doesn’t seem like it right now. we’ll be connected in death: one consciousness, one soul. we can share each others pain and anguish; all our neglect, all our loneliness, all our suffering. we can share each others joy and bliss; all our love, all our connection, all our comfort. i can’t wait.
